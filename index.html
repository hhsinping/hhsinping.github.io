Hsin-Ping Huang

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in the Vision and Learning Lab at University of California, Merced, under the supervision of Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang. Previously, I received my M.S. in Computer Science from The University of Texas at Austin and my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University.

My research interests lie in computer vision and machine learning, with a focus on video generation and 3D content creation. I am fortunate to intern at Google with Deqing Sun, Yu-Chuan Su, Hexiang Hu, Lu Jiang, Charles Herrmann, and Yaojie Liu, and to receive advice from Hung-Yu Tseng and Jia-Bin Huang.

I am honored to be a finalist for the Meta PhD Research Fellowship.

I will be seeking full-time positions in late 2024 to 2025.
